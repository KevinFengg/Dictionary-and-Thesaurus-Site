Help

The front page consists of the most searched words. Hovering over a dictionary or thesaurus side tab will extend the tab, revealing a search bar that will allow users to search a word. Searching words in the dictionary section will provide list all different definitions, provide a number of sentence examples, and allow the user to play an example of its audio pronounciation. Searching for words in the thesaurus will provide synonyms and antonyms. Additionally, when in the dictionary or thesaurus page, a search bar will be provided to allow users to search other words in the same section.
